Welcome!
A quick tour of your Book Crawl companion — it only takes a minute.
By Stores
See every bookstore on the crawl. Tap a store to expand it and see which books on your list are likely there. Use the ≡ handle to reorder stops to match your route.
By Books
See your full wish list in one place sorted by priority. Tap + to add a book with its title, author, genre, and which stores likely carry it.
Marking off in By Stores
Standing in a store? Tap the checkbox next to a book to mark it found. It automatically records which store you bought it at and fades the row out.
Marking off in By Books
In By Books view, use the store dropdown on each book to select where you bought it. Choosing a store marks the book as found and credits that store as visited.
Store complete!
Once a store is visited — either by buying a book there or tapping the circle ○ — the card turns lime green and confetti falls. The stores left count drops by one.
